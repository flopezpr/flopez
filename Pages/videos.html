This is one of my end of the year videos. I feel that it's easy to forget all of the good times we have every year so I wanted to capture as many as I could to be able to look back at them and smile. It's a great feeling to see not just how I've grown but how my family and friends have grown as well.

I go see my family in Puerto Rico as often as possible. I think it's important to remember where you came from so you can appreciate where you are in your journey. I'm forever grateful that I come from such a culturally rich island full of creative and resilient people.

So, in 2022 I caught the camping fever. After watching hundreds(if not thousands) of overlanding, van life, and Japanese camping videos, I decided to take the leap and go on a roadtrip around the country. I borrowed my dad's SUV because my sedan just was not going to cut it and I filled it with my solar fridge, power station, food, tablet, and my trusty rice cooker. I drove from Oklahoma to Arkansas, spent a night in a beautiful forest in Tennessee, spend a night in a camper AirBnB in Atlanta where I went to my first solo concert, then headed back to Arkansas and spent two nights in a secluded forest in Fairfield Bay where I met one of the coolest person. He was living off grid with his dog and it was just great hearing his stories. I would love to go back out and do this again.
